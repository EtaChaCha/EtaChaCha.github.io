### Experiences
During my university years, I took part in various student organizations, practical activities, and interesting events, which helped me grow in many aspects.

- I joined the **New Media Operations Department** of my faculty, where I was mainly responsible for **operating the official WeChat account**. I regularly typeset and published articles. This experience greatly improved my **writing skills**, taught me to use professional typesetting tools to make articles more visually appealing, and enhanced my **aesthetic ability**. Most importantly, I learned how to **communicate, coordinate and cooperate** with others effectively.

- I was a member of the **Astronomy Club**, where I learned to **operate astronomical telescopes** for stargazing. Regular club lectures also helped me gain systematic knowledge in **astronomy**.

- I joined the **University Museum Guide Team**. Through this experience, I learned knowledge about **geology, jewelry, paleontology and aerospace**. More importantly, I practiced and improved my **public speaking and expression skills** by explaining these contents clearly to visitors. In my spare time, I volunteered as a guide for one to two hours, which also contributed to my volunteer hours — a win-win experience.

- One of my most memorable experiences was taking the **Field Survival Course**. Under the guidance of the teacher, we hiked, climbed rocks, made fires, cooked meals and camped outdoors. Every activity was exciting and rewarding. This experience taught me practical **field survival skills** and further inspired my **curiosity and passion for exploring nature**.
